About DrupalCon London

DrupalCon is an international event that brings together the people who use, develop, design, and support the Drupal platform. DrupalCon London will be held 22nd-26th August, 2011, at the Fairfield Halls, London.

The conference features dozens of curated sessions and panels from some of the most influential people and brightest minds within the Drupal community and beyond, as well as countless opportunities for networking, sprints, informal conversations, and much, much more.

About this application

The DrupalCon mobile application lets you view session schedules, descriptions, maps, and presenter biographies from the palm of your hand, as well as live updates from the official DrupalCon Twitter feed. The DrupalCon mobile application for iPhone and Android was developed by Kasper Friis Christensen (falkendk) and Jeff Veit of Tanasity, building on the work of Jen Simmons, and Pat Teglia of Palantir.net using the Appcelerator Titanium mobile framework. The code for this application is available at https://github.com/KasperChristensen/drupalcon_london_mobile.
